Sound Guessing Game
Grandparents: 0
Kids: 0
  1. Click Shuffle Sounds
  2. Pick a sound at random
  3. Listen and guess what the sound is, then click "Reveal answer" by the sound you clicked
  4. If you get it right, click the +1 button by your age group
  5. Switch! Kids, give your grandparents a turn or vice versa.
  6. Do this for all the sounds until there are none left
  7. Shuffle sounds when done so nobody else can see the answers!
